WHAT IS FINANCE!!
Finance is the study of wealth distribution.
It is the method by which we can distribute wealth in the deficit unit from the surplus unit.
Types of Finance:
1. Corporate Finance
2. Public Finance
3. Personal Finance
Job sector
1. Chief Financial Officer(CFO);
2. Capital market Analyst;
3. Banking
4. Portfolio Manager
